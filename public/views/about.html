About Study Buddy

"When you run into something interesting, drop everything else and study it."

-B.F. Skinner

When you need to get focused, where do you go? Do you need silence or can you zone out in the midst of a flurry of activity? Do you fuel your study sessions with coffee? Where do you go when you need to work with a group?

Does thinking about all of this stress you out? It sounds like you need our wonderful tool, Study Buddy!

Study Buddy is a location-based search engine that makes finding a venue for studying and working remotely a simple, streamlined experience. Whether you’re studying for a big exam, perfecting prose in the latest draft of your novel, or just need a change of scenery while working remotely, Study Buddy takes the guesswork out of locating the appropriate space that promotes productivity. Study Buddy provides a customizable experience in which users can target criteria like venue size, noise levels and outlet availability to filter the results found in their area and locate a study venue that best suits their needs.

Now doesn’t that make you feel better about focusing?
